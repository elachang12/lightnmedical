Welcome New Patients

Become a Patient at Lightn Medical

We're excited to welcome you to our healthcare family! At Lightn Medical, we provide comprehensive primary care services for adults 18 years and older in the Austin area. Our team is dedicated to making your healthcare experience comfortable, convenient, and personalized.

We're currently accepting new patients and offer same-day appointments to ensure you receive timely care when you need it most.

How to Become a Patient

1

Schedule Your First Appointment

Contact us to schedule your initial visit. You can call our office or use our online scheduling system. We'll help you choose the right provider for your needs.

2

Complete Patient Forms

Before your visit, please complete our new patient forms available through our patient portal. This helps us prepare for your appointment and saves you time.

3

Bring Required Documents

Bring your insurance card, photo ID, and any previous medical records to your first appointment. Arrive 15 minutes early to complete any remaining paperwork.

What to Bring to Your First Visit

  • Valid photo identification (driver's license, passport, or state ID)
  • Current insurance card(s)
  • List of current medications (including dosages)
  • Previous medical records (if available)
  • Payment method for any copays or deductibles
  • Completed patient forms (if not submitted online)
